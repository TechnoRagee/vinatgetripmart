✦ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a tour with VintageTripmart?

Simply fill out our Travel Query Form, and our team will reach out within 24 hours with a custom itinerary and pricing details.

Do you offer group and corporate travel packages?

Absolutely! We specialize in group bookings (10-500 people) and corporate retreats. Contact us directly for special group pricing.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 30+ days before departure receive a full refund. 15-29 days: 50% refund. Less than 15 days: no refund. Travel insurance is strongly recommended.

Do you assist with visa applications?

Yes! We provide complete visa documentation guidance and assistance for all major international destinations. Visa fees are not included in tour packages.
